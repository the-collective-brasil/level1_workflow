DIE reflection — three guided stages
D
Describe
GPT guides teacher to produce an objective, factual description of what they observed. One question at a time. Coaches away from interpretation.
I
Interpret
GPT guides a tentative developmental interpretation. References exposure phase and calibration notes silently. Suggests 2–3 relevant LO codes from the rubric.
E
Evaluate
For each suggested LO code, GPT guides teacher to assign a level 1–5 with a rationale, using rubric descriptors from the group profile.
GPT-guided The conversation is structured but not scripted. GPT adapts based on what the teacher describes. Rubric descriptors are referenced silently — the teacher never sees raw spreadsheet data.

Apps Script No new row is appended. The L1 row created at upload is updated in place. Columns G (LO_code), I (describe), J (interpret), K (evaluate), L (assessment) are filled.
Apps Script: upgrades activity_tracker
U → D for today's date · writes D directly if no U exists
Apps Script D = full DIE reflection completed. Never writes both U and D — D always replaces U on the same date.

Appends [dd/mm] Level X — justification to student's LO cell
Apps Script Each LO evaluation is stacked chronologically inside the cell — not overwritten. This is the raw material for the end-of-semester report.
Apps Script: updates Student Assessment
Adds date + score entry · checks stagnation
Stagnation check If the last 3 assessment scores for this student show no improvement, a flag is written. Coordinator is alerted via Make.com — not the teacher.
Apps Script: checks badges + coverage gaps
Awards badges · calculates top 3 gaps live from learning_log
Coverage gaps Calculated on demand — never written to the sheet. Returned in the response payload for the GPT to relay to the teacher.
